## 2023-05-01
Dave opened his own car maintenance shop on or before 1 May 2023. This was a major dream of his that he has finally achieved. The shop is a place where he can work on cars, which he finds very satisfying. He works on all kinds of cars at the shop - from regular maintenance to full restorations of classic cars. The shop keeps him busy and happy. Dave is passionate about working on cars and finds it rewarding to see the transformation of vehicles and to help people keep their cars in good condition. He shared photos of the shop with Calvin.
