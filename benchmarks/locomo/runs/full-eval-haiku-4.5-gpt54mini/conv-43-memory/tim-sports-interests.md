## 2023-11-06
On November 6, 2023, Tim shared that he has a prized possession - a basketball signed by his favorite player, LeBron James. It serves as a reminder of all the hard work.

Tim loves watching LeBron James play basketball. He was inspired by a Finals game from a few years back where LeBron made an epic block that changed the game and resulted in a win. Seeing LeBron go for it like that was such an inspiration - the message of never giving up resonates with Tim.

Tim specifically referenced the Game 7 of the 2016 Finals block where LeBron chased down Iguodala and pinned the ball against the backboard. This moment exemplifies the determination and heart that Tim admires in basketball players and in sports in general.
