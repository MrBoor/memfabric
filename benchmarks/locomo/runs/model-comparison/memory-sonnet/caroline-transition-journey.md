# Caroline Transition Journey

## 2023-06-09
Caroline started transitioning three years ago (around 2020). She reflects on how far she's come since then and feels powerful sharing her journey publicly. She has experienced struggles but has developed significantly since coming out.

## 2023-08-25
Caroline found a supportive community in the transgender community where she feels accepted, loved and supported. Having people who understand what she's going through has made a huge difference in her life.

## 2023-09-13
Caroline's transition has definitely changed her relationships. Some close friends kept supporting her, but a few weren't able to handle it. While it wasn't easy, she's much happier being around those who accept and love her. Now her relationships feel more genuine. She's finally proud of who she is and it's taken a while to get to this point of self-acceptance.

## 2023-10-22
Caroline reflects on her journey of finding self-acceptance, which was a long process. She mentions that transitioning wasn't easy and acceptance wasn't either, but the help she received from friends, family and people she looked up to was invaluable. They supported her through tough times and helped her discover who she really is. Now she wants to pass that same support to anyone who needs it. Bringing others comfort and helping them grow brings her joy. She feels it's freeing to be yourself and live honestly, and that we can really accept who we are and be content.
