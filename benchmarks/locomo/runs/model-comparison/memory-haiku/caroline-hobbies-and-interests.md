# Caroline Hobbies And Interests

## Art & Painting
Started creating art at age 17. Finds it empowering, cathartic, and therapeutic. Art shows things hard to put into words. Uses painting to explore identity, transition, and changing body. Created paintings: "Embracing Identity" (about self-acceptance), self-portrait in blue (liberating/empowering), sunset painting from beach visit (vivid colors, serenity), painting with red/blue representing binary gender system (smashing rigid thinking, loving authentic self). Draws flowers frequently - appreciates nature. Interested in trying pottery. Paintings shown at LGBTQ art shows.

## Music
Plays acoustic guitar (started ~2018, ~5 years). Uses it to express emotions and escape into feelings. Favorite song: "Brave" by Sara Bareilles - about courage and fighting for what's right. Music brings people together, is cathartic and uplifting.

## Other Interests
Learning piano (creative outlet). Loves reading - books guide and motivate her. Favorite book: "Becoming Nicole" by Amy Ellis Nutt (trans girl story, gave her hope and taught self-acceptance). Favorite color: blue (relaxing). Appreciates flower symbolism: sunflowers (warmth/happiness), white roses (love/beauty).

## 2023-10-13
Recently trying abstract art - finds it freeing to put feelings on canvas without too much planning. Attended a transgender poetry reading on 6 October 2023 (last Friday). The event was powerful and empowering - transgender people shared their stories through poetry in a safe space for self-expression and celebrating identities. The room had electric energy and support. Inspired by the event, created art with posters featuring pride and strength. Drew a piece that stands for freedom and being real - a nudge to stay true to herself and embrace her womanhood.
