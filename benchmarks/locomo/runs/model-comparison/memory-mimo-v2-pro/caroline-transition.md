# Caroline Transition

## 2023-06-09
Started transitioning three years ago (around June 2020). Has come a long way since beginning her transition. Identifies as transgender. Feels powerful sharing her journey publicly.

## 2023-08-14
Art has allowed her to explore her transition and her changing body. It's been a great way to work through things she's going through. Teaches her to accept the beauty of imperfections.

## 2023-08-25
Finding the transgender community where she's accepted, loved, and supported has meant a lot — made a huge difference having people who understand what she's going through. Her art (murals, stained glass) is deeply connected to her trans identity and journey.

## 2023-09-13
Made a painting showing her path as a trans woman. Red and blue represent the binary gender system; mixed colors symbolize smashing rigid thinking. The painting is a reminder to love her authentic self — it took a while to get here but she's finally proud of who she is.

## 2023-10-22
Reflects that transitioning wasn't easy and self-acceptance was a long process. Help from friends, family, and people she looked up to was invaluable — they boosted her through tough times and helped her discover who she really is.
