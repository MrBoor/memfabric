# Melanie Family Traditions

## 2023-07-20
Family has an annual summer camping trip tradition. They roast marshmallows, tell stories around the campfire, and enjoy each other's company. It's the highlight of their summer.

## 2023-10-20
Values camping with family as a chance to be present and together. They bond over stories, campfires, and nature. Loves waking up to the sound of birds and the smell of fresh air — describes it as refreshing her soul. Finds peace and serenity in nature with family.
