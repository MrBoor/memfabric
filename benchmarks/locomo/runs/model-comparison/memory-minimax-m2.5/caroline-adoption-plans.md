## 2023-10-13
Caroline contacted her mentor for adoption advice. She is ready to start her journey to become a mom and share her love and family. It's a great feeling for her - she's stoked to start this new chapter. It's been a dream to adopt and provide a safe, loving home for kids who need it.

## 2023-10-13
Caroline shared adoption tips with Melanie: do research and find an adoption agency or lawyer - they'll help with the process and provide all the info. Gather documents like references, financial info, and medical checks. Don't forget to prepare emotionally since the wait can be hard. It's all worth it in the end though.

Her vision is to build her own family and give a roof over kids who haven't had that before. For her, adoption is a way of giving back and showing love and acceptance. Her dream is to create a safe and loving home for these kids.
