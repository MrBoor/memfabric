# Caroline Books

## 2023-07-12
Caroline loves reading - books guide her, motivate her, and help her discover who she is. They are a huge part of her journey. Her favorite book is "Becoming Nicole" by Amy Ellis Nutt, a true story about a trans girl and her family. It made her feel connected and gave her hope for her own path. She recommends it to others. The book taught her self-acceptance, how to find support, and that tough times don't last - hope and love exist.
