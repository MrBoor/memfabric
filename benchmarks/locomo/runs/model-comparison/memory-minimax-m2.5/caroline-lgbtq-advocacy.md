Caroline gave a talk at her school about her transgender journey on June 2, 2023, encouraging students to get involved in the LGBTQ community. She shared her personal struggles and how much she has developed since coming out three years ago. The event strengthened her commitment to being a voice for the trans community.

Caroline attended an LGBTQ conference on July 10, 2023. She felt totally accepted in the welcoming environment and got to meet and connect with people who've gone through similar journeys. She wants to help fight for trans rights and spread awareness.

The best moment was seeing her mentee's face light up when they saw the support.
