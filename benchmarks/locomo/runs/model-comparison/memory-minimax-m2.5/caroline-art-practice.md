# Caroline Art Practice

## 2023-08-14
Caroline is an artist who uses her creative work to represent inclusivity and diversity. She uses her art to speak up for the LGBTQ+ community and push for acceptance. Her art is about expressing her trans experience - it's her way of showing her story and helping people understand the trans community.

She created a painting called "Embracing Identity" which is about finding comfort and love in being yourself. The woman in the painting represents the journey of acceptance. Her aim was to show warmth, love, and self-acceptance.

Art has allowed Caroline to explore her transition and her changing body. It's been a great way to work through stuff she's going through. She loves that it teaches her to accept the beauty of imperfections.

## 2023-09-13
Caroline started creating art around age 17. She finds it empowering and cathartic - it's amazing how art can show things that are hard to put into words. Painting and drawing helped her express her feelings and explore her gender identity. Creating art was really important during her transition - it helped her understand and accept herself.

Caroline created a painting showing her path as a trans woman. The red and blue colors represent the binary gender system, and the mix of colors represents smashing that rigid thinking. It's a reminder to love her authentic self - it's taken a while to get here but she's finally proud of who she is. She hasn't done pottery yet but is interested in trying it.
