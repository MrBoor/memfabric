# Melanie Art Hobbies

## 2023-05-08
Melanie enjoys painting as a hobby. She painted a lake sunrise last year (2022) which is special to her. She finds painting is a fun way to express her feelings and get creative, and a great way to relax after a long day.

## 2023-07-02
Melanie signed up for a pottery class on July 2, 2023. She describes pottery as therapy - it helps her express herself and get creative. She finds making pottery calming and considers it a huge part of her life, not just a hobby. She made a black and white bowl in her class which she is proud of. Clay brings her joy and helps her express emotions.

## 2023-07-15
Melanie took her kids to a pottery workshop on July 7, 2023. They all made their own pots - the kids loved getting their hands dirty and making something with clay. It was special watching their creativity and imagination come to life.

Family painting together is a favorite activity, especially nature-inspired paintings. They found lovely flowers for their latest painting from July 8-9, 2023.

## 2023-08-17
