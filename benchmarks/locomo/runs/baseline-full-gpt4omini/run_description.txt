# Full-Context Baseline: All 10 Conversations

**Date:** 2026-03-21
**Dataset:** LoCoMo (all 10 conversations, 1,540 questions excl. adversarial)
**Method:** Full conversation dumped into LLM context (no memory system)
**Query model:** GPT-4o-mini
**Judge model:** GPT-4o-mini
**Total runtime:** ~72 min

---

## Results

MemFabric wins overall by 1.5pp, but the story is in the categories:

| Category    | Baseline | MemFabric | Delta   | Winner    | Count |
|-------------|----------|-----------|---------|-----------|-------|
| **Overall** | 77.2%    | **78.7%** | +1.5pp  | MemFabric | 1,540 |
| Open-domain | **90.1%** | 84.5%    | -5.6pp  | Baseline  | 841   |
| Single-hop  | 76.2%    | 76.2%    | 0       | Tie       | 282   |

| Multi-hop   | 50.2%    | **68.2%** | +18.1pp | MemFabric | 321   |

## Analysis

### Why the baseline is stronger than expected

The published full-context baseline (~73%) used older models. GPT-4o-mini
is much better at finding information in long contexts, pushing the baseline
to 77.2%. This narrows MemFabric's advantage.

### Where each system excels

1. **Baseline wins on open-domain (90.1% vs 84.5%)** — with the full
   conversation available, the model can find specific details and combine
   them with world knowledge more effectively.

2. **MemFabric wins decisively on multi-hop (+18.1pp) and temporal (+12.5pp)**
   — organized memory files make cross-session reasoning easier. The baseline
   struggles when facts are scattered across a 20K-token conversation.

3. **Single-hop is a tie (76.2%)** — both approaches handle simple lookups
   equally well.

### Implications

The baseline is a strong competitor when conversations fit in context. MemFabric's
value increases with:
- More sessions requiring cross-session reasoning
- Questions requiring temporal ordering of events
- Conversations that exceed the model's effective attention span

For real-world use where conversations grow over months/years and far exceed
context limits, MemFabric's approach becomes essential — the baseline simply
cannot scale.
