# Deborah Family

## Parents
- Mother passed away a few years ago. Father passed away January 25, 2023 (sudden death, left family shell-shocked). Parents' wedding was in 1993.
- Mother: had a soothing voice, sang lullabies to Deborah as a child. Loved sitting on a bench near the window every morning. Hobbies included reading (read by window nightly), travel, art (believed art connects us emotionally), and cooking (made amazing meals; special dish filled house with memorable smell, brought family together). Passion for flowers — they always made her happy. Taught Deborah to appreciate simple things by taking slow, finding beauty and joy. Was Deborah's biggest yoga fan and would attend her classes. Made pineapple birthday cakes for Deborah as a child — favorite childhood memory.
- Has an amulet from her mother; brings it when visiting a spot by water near mom's old house for reflection and comfort.

## Mother's Old House
- Holds many memories. Has a special bench by the window where mother loved to sit. Deborah sometimes sits there to feel connected. Visited July 2023 and September 2023 — house represents mother's strength and love. Brought flowers there for her mom.

## Husband
- Has a husband. Values love and openness in their marriage; being there for each other keeps them close and happy.

## Other
- Reconnected with mom's old friends (September 15, 2023) — shared stories and old photos, emotional but comforting, learned new things about her mother.
- Believes remembering loved ones is important; finds comfort knowing their influence still guides her.
