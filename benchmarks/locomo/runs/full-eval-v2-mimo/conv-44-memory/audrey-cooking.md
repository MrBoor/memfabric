# Audrey Cooking

## 2023-07-03
Loves cooking. Favorite recipe is Chicken Pot Pie — a family recipe that's been around for years; reminds her of her grandma's kitchen and conversations at the table. Also loves Roasted Chicken — inspired by Mediterranean flavors, made with chicken, garlic, lemon, and herbs; her favorite comfort meal. Cooking is an escape and a way to let creativity flow. Loves throwing on music, pouring a glass of wine, and going with the flow in the kitchen — finds it therapeutic. Go-to ingredient is garlic — loves the smell and taste it adds to dishes.
