# John Community And Politics

Interested in local politics. Focus: improving education and infrastructure. Motivated by childhood experience of seeing how lack of education and crumbling infrastructure negatively affected his neighborhood. Involved in a school funding project that enabled repairs and renovations. Helped renovate a rundown community center back home (2022); community came together; center now busy and thriving.

Running for office. Motivated by impact he can make through politics.
