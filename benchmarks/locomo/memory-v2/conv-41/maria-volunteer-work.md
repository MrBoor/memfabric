# Maria Volunteer Work

Started volunteering at homeless shelter around August 2022 after witnessing family struggling on streets. Reached out to shelter asking if they needed volunteers; they said yes. Volunteering is fulfilling but tough; growing need can be overwhelming. Believes little acts of kindness have big effect and that listening makes biggest impact.

## Key Volunteer Moments & Connections
- 2023-01-28: Comforted sad 8-year-old girl at shelter kids' event; they laughed together.
- 2023-02-05: Met David (homeless); connected him with nearby organization offering housing and support.
- 2023-02-25: Met Jean at shelter going through divorce, job loss, homelessness but remained optimistic and resilient; Jean's resilience inspired Maria.
- 2023-04-07: Participated in 5K charity run for homeless shelter; gave talks; organized meal for residents; found it fulfilling to bring people together.
- 2023-06-27: Had memorable moment playing with kids at shelter—someone sad for months suddenly laughed.
- 2023-07-03: Met kids at shelter whose smiles warmed her heart. Received letter from shelter resident Laura expressing gratitude for impact Maria made on her life.
- 2023-08-09: Received a medal for her volunteer work; found experience humbling.
- 2023-08-11: Volunteered at shelter front desk; seeing smiles on residents' faces when they received food or a bed made her feel good; reinforced belief that she has power to make a difference.
