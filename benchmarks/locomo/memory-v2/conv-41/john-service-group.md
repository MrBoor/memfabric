Rescued a family from a burning building. Felt chaotic initially but team pulled together. Got surge of energy and purpose. Adrenaline rush but fulfilling knowing they made a difference. Team has become like family—trusts and relies on them. Different skills and talents all contribute to serving and protecting community. Bond similar to military experience. Finds it fulfilling and meaningful.
