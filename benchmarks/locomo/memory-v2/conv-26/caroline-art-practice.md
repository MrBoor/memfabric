# Caroline Art Practice

## Art Style & Themes
Started creating art at age 17. Finds it empowering and cathartic. Main focus is expressing her trans experience - showing her story and helping people understand the trans community. Uses art to represent inclusivity and diversity. Art is healing and helps with self-discovery and acceptance journey. Speaks up for LGBTQ+ community through her art.

## Favorite Subjects & Colors
Blue is her favorite color - makes her feel relaxed. Appreciates flower symbolism: sunflowers (warmth, happiness), white roses (love, beauty). Drawing flowers is one of her favorite activities. Appreciates nature and enjoys sharing it through art.

## Notable Paintings
- "Embracing Identity" (2023-08-14): About finding comfort and love in being yourself. Woman in painting represents journey of acceptance. Shows warmth, love, and self-acceptance.
- Red and blue painting (2023-09-13): Represents binary gender system with mixed colors showing rejection of rigid thinking. Reminder to love authentic self.
- Self-portrait with blue (2023-08-23): Felt liberated and empowered while painting.
- Sunset painting (2023-08-25): Inspired by beach visit on 18 August 2023. Captures sun dipping below horizon with vivid colors, conveys peace and serenity.
- Abstract painting (2023-10-13): Recently started trying abstract art. Finds it freeing to put feelings on canvas without too much plan.
- Freedom and womanhood piece (2023-10-13): Drew after attending transgender poetry reading on 6 October 2023. Stands for freedom and being real - nudge to stay true to herself and embrace her womanhood.

## Art Shows & Community
Had painting for LGBTQ art show inspired by visit to LGBTQ center - captures unity and strength of community with vivid colors and unified composition. Putting together LGBTQ art show (September 2023) to display paintings featuring LGBTQ artists and their talents, aiming to spread understanding and acceptance.

## Art as Therapy
Art was really important during her transition - helped her understand and accept herself. Uses art as way to work through experiences. Art teaches her to accept beauty of imperfections. Painting helps her explore her identity and be true to herself. Art gives her joy and helps her express feelings and freeze gorgeous moments. Shows things that are hard to put into words.
