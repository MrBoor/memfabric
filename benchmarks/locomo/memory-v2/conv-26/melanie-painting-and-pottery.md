# Melanie Painting And Pottery

## Art Background & Philosophy
Has been doing art for seven years. Found her real muses: painting and pottery. Both are calming, satisfying, and therapeutic. Views art as both creative outlet and form of therapy. Uses art to express emotions and get feelings out. Painting helps her express feelings and brings happiness and fulfillment.

Pottery is huge part of her life, not just hobby - helps her express emotions and brings joy. Describes pottery as both sanctuary and source of comfort. Obsessed with bright colors and patterns in pottery. Each stroke of paint carries part of her.

Notable pottery projects: black and white bowl (July 2023), colorful bowl with patterns (July 2023), pottery plate (24 August 2023), pottery bowls with cool designs (September 2023).

## Painting
Paints as hobby. Created lake sunrise painting last year that is special to her. Uses painting to express feelings, be creative, and relax after long days.

Favorite painting subjects: landscapes, still life, animals (particularly horses). Loves painting animals because it's peaceful and special. Appreciates grace of horses and captures that in art.

Paints nature-inspired paintings with kids as bonding activity. Recently completed painting with kids on 14 July 2023 featuring bold colors highlighting beauty of nature. Painting together allows them to chat about nature and appreciate small things in life.

Recent paintings: sunset-inspired painting (around 6 October 2023) with colors that make her feel calm; abstract painting with blue streaks (October 2023) to show tranquility. Blue calms her, so wanted painting to have serene vibe while still having lots of vibrant colors.
