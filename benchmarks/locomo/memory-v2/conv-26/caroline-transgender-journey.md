# Caroline Transgender Journey And Identity

## 2023-06-27
Motivated to pursue counseling by her own journey and support received. Passionate about creating safe spaces for people to grow and helping others through similar journeys.

## 2023-07-15
Attended pride parade (late June 2023). Felt accepted, happy, and grateful. Transition was best part of her journey - freeing to express herself authentically. Proud of how far she's come; transition important for mental health and peace.

## 2023-08-25
Had a negative hiking experience on 18 August 2023. Got into a bad spot with some people during the hike. The experience bothered her, so she attempted to apologize to them. Demonstrates her commitment to resolving conflicts and taking responsibility. Finding a community where she is accepted, loved, and supported has made a huge difference. Created a rainbow flag mural reflecting the courage and strength of the trans community. The eagle in the mural symbolizes freedom and pride, representing her own resilience and that of others. Created a stained glass window for a local church showing time changing lives, representing her own journey as a transgender woman and emphasizing accepting growth and change. Discovered a rainbow sidewalk for Pride Month in her neighborhood while walking. The vibrant and welcoming sidewalk reminded her that love and acceptance are everywhere, even in unexpected places.
## 2023-09-13
Transition was transformative. Art was really important during transition - helped her understand and accept herself. Finally proud of who she is. Journey has changed her relationships - some close friends kept supporting her, but a few weren't able to handle it. Now her relationships feel more genuine because she's around people who accept and love her. Much happier being around those who accept her authentic self.

## 2023-10-22
Finding self-acceptance was a long process. Transition was not easy, and acceptance didn't come easily either. Received invaluable help from friends, family, and mentors who supported her through tough times and helped her discover who she really is. Now feels empowered and ready to offer love and support to others who need it. Bringing others comfort and helping them grow brings her joy. Feels grateful to share her journey and help others with theirs. Being yourself is freeing and allows you to live honestly and accept who you are.
