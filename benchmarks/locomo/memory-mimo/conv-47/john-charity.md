# John Charity

## 2022-05-07
Organized a CS:GO tournament with friends to raise money for charity. The event was successful with good turnout and they raised significant funds. Main beneficiary was a dog shelter near where he lives. After helping the shelter, used remaining money to buy groceries and cook food for the homeless. Plans to organize more charity events in the future — wants to combine gaming interests with helping the community. Finds it rewarding to bring people together for friendly competition while supporting good causes.

## 2022-10-30
Held a gaming tournament with his gaming pals on October 30, 2022. Played Fortnite, Overwatch, and Apex Legends. Raised money for a children's hospital. Everyone was competitive and had a blast. Describes combining gaming with a good cause as awesome.
