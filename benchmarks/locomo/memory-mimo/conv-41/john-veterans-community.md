# John Veterans & Community Service

## 2023-01-01
With service group: visited homeless shelter to give out food/supplies. Organized a toy drive for kids in need.

## 2023-02-05
Helped with community food drive for unemployed residents. Overwhelming volunteer response.

## 2023-05-12
Hosted veterans' party. Veterans shared stories, found camaraderie. Heartwarming event with new friendships.

## 2023-05-20
Working on veterans support project with petition. Passionate about veterans' rights.

## 2023-07-03
Participated in marching event for veterans' rights. Always had great respect for the military. Motivated by others sharing same values. Believes we must support veterans however we can.

## 2023-07-10
Visited a veteran's hospital. Met an elderly veteran named Samuel who shared inspiring and heartbreaking stories. The experience was eye-opening — seeing veterans' resilience filled him with hope. Reminded him why he wanted to join the military.

## 2023-07-31
Joined a fire-fighting brigade. Inspired by Maria's work at the homeless shelter to find his own way to give back. Training was intense and taxing but rewarding. First call-out was around July 23, 2023 — responded to a situation, team worked together to save a family from a burning building. Describes the experience as chaotic but fulfilling.

## 2023-08-06
Organized a 5K charity run in the neighborhood to raise money for veterans and their families. Successfully raised funds. Had to get sponsors, coordinate with the city, and spread the word. Getting sponsors was difficult but they ended up with awesome sponsors that made the event a hit.

## 2023-08-06
Worked with a local organization that helps victims of domestic abuse. Raised awareness and funds at an event for the cause. Recognizes domestic abuse as a horrible issue and believes it's important to support organizations fighting against it.
## 2023-08-16
Fire-fighting brigade organized a donation drive. Raised donations including canned food, toiletries, and clothes. The drive took two hours of hard work. Donations contributed to getting a brand new fire truck for the local fire station.
